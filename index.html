Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really free?
Yes. We are currently in Beta, allowing high-potential candidates to use the platform at no cost while we refine our matching algorithms.
How accurately does it detect salary?
If a listing doesn't explicitly state salary, we cross-reference the company size, location, and role level against a database of 500k+ recent data points to provide an estimated range with ±10% accuracy.
Does my current employer know?
Never. We operate as a private agent. You can also explicitly blacklist specific domains (like your current employer's) to ensure you never match with them.
